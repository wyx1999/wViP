Overview

The wViP is an online service for easy-to-use word cloud visualization of biological profiles function. The wViP pipeline is extremely intuitive and both supporting keyboard input and files uploading. With diverse built-in and user-defined options, users can easily complete the natural language processing steps and illustrate their preferred word cloud through a few clicks.

Gallery

wViP could be applied in various scenarios according to user's requirement. Here we present four application scenarios applicable to wViP, including research career summary, biological finding summarization, scientific hotspot highlighting, the journal's publication profile, and enrichment analysis visualization (Hover over the image to see more information).
